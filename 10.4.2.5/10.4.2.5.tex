\item The altitude of a right triangle is 7 cm less than its base.if the hypotenuse is 13 cm, find the other two sides.\\
\solution \\
Given Data:\\
hypotenuse = 13\\
Let,
base = x\\
altitude = x-7\\
\begin{align}
{base}^2 + {altitude}^2 &= {hypotenuse}^2\\
{x}^2 + {(x-7)}^2 &= {13}^2\\
{x}^2 + {x}^2 + {49}-{14x} &= {169}\\
2{x}^2- 14x- 120 &= 0\\
2({x}^2 - 7x - 60) &= 0\\
(x^2 - 12x + 5x - 60)&= 0\\
x(x-12) + 5(x-12) &= 0\\
(X+5)(x-12) &= 0\\
x=12 or -5
\end{align}
sides cannot be negative so x = 12\\
so, base = 12cm,\\
altitude = 12-7= 5cm\\

\end{enumerate}
